Put on headset

  • Loosen strap
  • Headset level on face
  • Strap on back of head
  • Tighten until snug

Battery strap

  • Plug in USB-C
  • Battery on back of head
  • Use dial to tighten

Headset buttons

  • Power = tap to wake
  • Power = hold to power off
  • Volume + / -

Glasses

  • Use glasses spacer (if available)
  • Put on headset slowly
  • Do not let frames touch lenses

Adjust clarity

  • Re-center headset on your face
  • Tighten strap until stable and comfortable
  • Turn the lens distance wheel until text looks clear

Take off / store

  • Loosen strap first
  • Lift headset up and forward
  • Place on a flat surface, lenses facing up
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
