Put on headset

  • Turn rear wheel to loosen first
  • Set headset level on your face
  • Place rear pad low on back of head
  • Tighten wheel until snug
  • Adjust top Velcro strap to lift face pressure

Glasses

  • Use the glasses spacer if needed
  • Put on headset slowly
  • Do not let your frames touch the lenses

Adjust clarity

  • Re-center headset on your face
  • Tighten until stable and comfortable
  • Turn the lens distance wheel until text is clear

Find the library

  • Press the Meta button
  • Open Library
  • Select the app you need

Recenter view

  • Face the direction you want as front
  • Hold the Meta button on the right controller
  • Release when the window snaps into your view

Switch MR and VR

  • Double-tap the side of the headset
  • This toggles between VR and passthrough (MR)
  • Double-tap again to return to your previous view
  • Use this as a quick safety check if you feel nauseous or need to see your surroundings

Quit an app

  • Press Meta button to open menu
  • Select the open app
  • Choose Quit

Update boundary

  • Open Quick settings
  • Select Boundary
  • Redraw before play if needed

Take off headset

  • Loosen strap first
  • Lift headset up and forward
  • Set it down with lenses facing up

Clean after use

  • Use microfiber cloth on lenses
  • Wipe face pad before handoff
  • No sprays or paper towels

Plug in for next person

  • Connect headset charging cable
  • Connect Elite Strap battery cable
  • Return controllers to charging spot
